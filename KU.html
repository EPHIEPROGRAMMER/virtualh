Serenity Men Hostel

The hostel is ideal for Kenyatte University (KU) Students. Located in Kiwanja (KM) behind Nyayo 6 near Adler hostel. The hostel can accommodate 56 students, it is fully furnished with ample security and a caretaker. For groups it is negotiable price:Ksh 3750




Safe Lounge Hostel

The hostel is ideal for students studying in the CBD or in Strathmore, Daystar and Riara University. Located in Nairobi West, on the crossroad of Muthaiti Avenue with Gandhi Road. It has the following features; - 4 sharing 9500. - 2 sharing 11000. - Single room 12000. Has Wi-Fi, hot shower, fully furnished with caretaker and breakfast offered price:Ksh 3750




Greenland Girls

Purely a girls' hostel with maximum security situated in Mamlaka area in Nairobi. Fresh water, electricity and WiFi available, spacious rooms with firm mattresses, bordering Technical University of Nairobi. Gated property. Meals available at a cost. Groups wishing to visit Nairobi can come and enjoy the cool breeze from the ocean. Charges are very manageable and reasonable. Charges: students Ksh. 3750, per month excluding food. For groups it is negotiable price:Ksh 3750
