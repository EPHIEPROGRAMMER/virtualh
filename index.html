Bac Girls Hostel

Purely a girls' hostel with maximum security situated in Mamlaka area in Nairobi. Fresh water, electricity and WiFi available, spacious rooms with firm mattresses, bordering Technical University of Nairobi. Gated property. Meals available at a cost. Groups wishing to visit Nairobi can come and enjoy the cool breeze from the ocean. Charges are very manageable and reasonable. Charges: students Ksh. 3750, per month excluding food. For groups it is negotiable price:Ksh 3750




Bereri Mens Hostel in Ngara

The hostel is ideal for students of Technical University, Africa Nazarene, UON, St. Paul’s universities etc. Located on Ngara Road. The hostel can accommodate 60 students, it has ample security and a caretaker. price:Ksh 6500


Baraka Girls Hostel in River Bank, South B

A hostel ideal for female students studying at Railway Training Institute, Technical University, Strathmore University, Riara University, Daystar University and other institutions This is a spacious 3 bedroom house located in South B near the Railway Training Institute. The house has been carefully tailored for a hostel Clean, quiet, safe and secure location Meals provided breakfast and dinner daily. Lunch included on weekends and public holidays Caretaker on site The amenities include:- Wi-Fi TV Electricity and water Hot shower Beds, mattresses and ironing board provided Dedicated study/reading room with shelves price:Ksh 11000

Nycis Hostel Karen

Nyci's Hostel offers modern, affordable self contained rooms a short walking distance to top universities in Karen such as KSL,JKUAT, CUEA and Tangaza college price : Ksh 12000

Buon Villa Hostel in Karen

Buon Villa hostels are located after Mbagathi River (Nkaimurunya) Behind P.E.F.A. Church. The hostels overlook Ongata Rongai town which creates a quiet, relaxing and a natural feel to the environment as one looks at the city from Karen, providing a sense of tranquillity within the city. The Hostels are strategically located to offer safe and reliable accommodation to Catholic university, Tangaza University, Cooperative University, JKUAT Karen University, Kenya School of law students, low budget tourists and also local clients from corporate companies. price :Ksh 5000

Azari Girls Hostel in Madaraka

Azari girls hostel is ideal for students studying at Strathmore University, Riara University, Daystar University and many other institution. This is a 3 bedroom apartment located in Madaraka 200 M from Strathmore University. It is a girls only hostel that houses a max of 5 students. The house has been carefully tailored for a hostel. The amenities include:- Free Wi-Fi Free electricity Free water Cable TV Fully stocked kitchen Mattresses and ironing board provided. Good security. Ample parking provided. Come and see for yourself and be the judge price : Ksh 16,000

Molly Hostel near Strathmore University

Located in Madaraka, Ole Sangale road. Facilities: Fully furnished apartment with free unlimited Wi-Fi, cable TV, furnished kitchen, furnished living rooms, and hot shower. price:Ksh 12,000
