The Mirvins Hostel

The Mirvins Hostels is a stress free environment with all the facilities that promote and support your study and academic growth. We offer budget accommodation at very competitive rates, for both long and short stay with the following facilities/services:
• Breakfast, afternoon coffee/tea and dinner served daily • Lunch during weekends and public holidays only • Accommodation is mainly special single rooms • Bed and mattress Accommodation fees - per semester (3months) From May 2016 price:Ksh 3750

St Claires Ladies Hostel

St. Claire’s is a ladies hostel. It is situated at Section 58 opposite state house, Nakuru. It’s accessible from town and other educational institutions that are within Nakuru town and its environs. It’s a walking distance from Nakuru bus stage. The hostel is managed by a sister hence high discipline levels.
Prices per room: Single (special) – ksh.10,000 per month Two sharing – ksh.8,000 per month Four sharing – ksh.4,500 per month Meals provided: Breakfast Supper
